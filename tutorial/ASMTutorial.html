ASM Tutorial

Follow the steps of this simple tutorial to get a quick run-through of how the ASM works. Click the links to view helpful screenshots.

Notice the two search boxes at the bottom. Let's use the left one to look up and add a class to the schedule.

  1. Click the Instructor field and select from the list Bentson, Randy.
  2. Click submit and a list of classes taught by Randy Bentson will show up in place of the search box.
  3. Click the title of CSCI 475, Operating Systems, to add it to the schedule.

The schedule should now look like this.

Now let's find another class to add, but this time we'll be adding it to more than one schedule.

  1. Click the Search tab to bring the search box back and click the clear button to reset the search fields.
  2. Click the Department field and select from the list CLASSICS.
  3. Click submit and a list of all the classes taught by the Classics department will show up in place of the search box.
  4. Click the check box next to Schedule 2 right above the Results box. The check boxes for both Schedule 1 and 2 should now be checked.
  5. Click the title of CLSC 210A, Greek Mythology, to add it to both Schedule 1 and 2.
  6. Click the tab for Schedule 2 at the top of the page to see the added class and then return to Schedule 1 .

Now we'll remove CSCI 475, Operating Systems, from the schedule.

  1. Click the red X in the top left corner of the class on the schedule to remove it.

The schedule should now look like this.

Finally, we'll print off the current schedule being viewed.

  1. Make sure Schedule 1 is being viewed.
  2. Click Print in the top right corner to print the current schedule.

Fun Trick:

Try filling in the schedule as much as possible. Can you fill all but 5 hours? Can you do even better? See if you can fill the entire thing!
